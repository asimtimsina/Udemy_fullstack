158. String Template Literals -SUPER USEFUL
Must use backticks ` for template literals.
let product = 'Artichoke';
let price = 2.25;
let qty = 5;

let msg = 'You bought ' + qty + ' ' + product + 's. Total: $' + price*qty;
msg -> "You bought 5 Artichokes. Total: $11.25"

let msg = `You bought ${qty} ${product}s. Total: $${price*qty}`;
msg -> "You bought 5 Artichokes. Total: $11.25"
